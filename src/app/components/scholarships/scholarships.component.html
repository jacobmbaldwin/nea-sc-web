Donate to the Scholarship Fund

At NEA Soccer Club, we believe that ALL players should have access to club soccer. That is why we created the NEA SC Scholarship Fund. We are asking anyone who is able and willing to donate to the fund so that we can provide this access.

Your donations would enable us to:

  • Support financially-challenged families by providing scholarships for kids to play soccer
  • Provide equipment for training players and building soccer fields
  • Promote soccer as a way to keep kids involved in a positive environment
